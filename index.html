Hello! We are from Girls Who Code (check the About Us page to find out more about us). We made this website to help students who are in the college application process or who just want to know more about a specific college. With our site, you are able to find more info about a college by just clicking on an interactive map containing the top 50 universities in the US. If you want to dive a little deeper, visit the More Info page to view the required GPA to get into a certain college, the required SAT and ACT scores, and the size of the college. Happy searching and we hope you enjoy our website!
